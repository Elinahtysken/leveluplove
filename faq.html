Frequently Asked Questions

How do you match participants for your events?

+

Our matching process is built on the principle of compatibility and shared values. During the selection phase, all participants complete a comprehensive questionnaire covering their interests, life goals, values, and relationship expectations. We believe in the similarity principle - that successful couples share fundamental commonalities in their outlook on life, ambitions, and core values.

Our experienced team carefully analyzes these responses to curate each event with participants who have genuine potential for meaningful connections. We don't just match based on superficial preferences, but on deeper compatibility factors that form the foundation of lasting relationships.

What criteria do you use to select participants for speed dating events?

+

We understand that our discerning clientele - both women and men - seek partners who embody success, wellness, and emotional maturity. Our selection process focuses on individuals who demonstrate:

  • Professional achievement: Established careers and clear professional direction
  • Personal wellness: Commitment to physical and mental health
  • Emotional intelligence: The ability to communicate effectively and show genuine interest in others
  • Confidence and security: Individuals who radiate self-assurance without arrogance
  • Relationship readiness: Genuine desire for serious, long-term commitment

This careful curation ensures that every participant meets someone truly worth their time and attention.

How do you choose your event venues?

+

Venue selection is crucial to creating the right atmosphere for meaningful connections. We exclusively choose locations that provide the perfect balance of:

  • Conversational comfort: Acoustics and layout that facilitate intimate, engaging dialogue
  • Sophisticated ambiance: Elegant, luxurious settings that reflect our clientele's refined tastes
  • Privacy and discretion: Exclusive spaces that ensure comfort and confidentiality
  • Professional service: Impeccable hospitality that allows you to focus entirely on making connections

From upscale private dining rooms to exclusive member clubs, every venue is carefully selected to enhance your experience and increase the likelihood of genuine connection.

What can I expect during a Level up Love event?

+

Each Level up Love event is uniquely designed with carefully planned activities that go beyond traditional speed dating. Our events feature:

  • Diverse interaction formats: From intimate conversation rounds to engaging ice-breaker activities
  • Structured yet natural flow: Professional facilitation that feels organic and comfortable
  • Quality time allocation: Sufficient time with each participant to gauge genuine interest and compatibility
  • Elegant refreshments: Premium dining or cocktail experiences integrated into the evening
  • Discreet follow-up process: Professional handling of mutual connections and next steps

We are meticulous in our planning to ensure every detail contributes to an exceptional experience where authentic connections can flourish naturally.
